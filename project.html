some historic details

Man's Dream to Take Off El Al is the old dream that only began to come true in the new age and was brought to fruition
   in the Kitty Hawk sands on December 17, 1903. -20 was the beginning of the age-long aviation and end-of-the-sun race center that
    encompassed thousands of visionaries and inventors for generations. 
The first aircraft is the kite invented in China in the fifth century BC. Later and independently, gliders and parachutes 
  developed in military use in Muslim Spain as well. Leonardo da Vinci sketched a number of aircraft models, but he did not actually
   try to build any of them.
It was the Mongolian brothers from France who managed to get into the air in 1783 with a hot air balloon. The same hot-air balloon 
  flight is considered to be the first time man has succeeded in getting into the air in the machine he built, but the Mongolian brothers' 
  hot-air balloon was light from the air and was at the mercy of the wind. After the flight of a pair of brothers, the inventors of the world
   sought a way to control the aircraft in flight. The first to succeed was Otto Lilienthal, the German who developed the steering principles
   used by airplanes to this day and demonstrated them on a glider he built in 1891.
Now all that's left is to add to this type of glider engine and finally take off in a machine that is under the pilot's absolute
  control. Otto Lilienthal worked hard for days on the construction of a motorized glider, but on one of his flights Glider crashed and
   Lilienthal couldn't see his dream come true. Lilienthal's dream and many others were fulfilled by the Wright brothers in 1903 when they
    took off on a motorized glider in the engine piston built by themselves.

Overseas equipment list - how can you not forget anything important? 

Whether it is your first flight abroad or the airport has become a routine landscape for you, always when you start packing,

Important documents 

Every overseas equipment list has to start with documents, most of which if you forget you can't reach your destination at all, then make sure this is the first category you handle!

  Passport + Passport Photo
  Printed airline ticket - Even if it is an electronic airline ticket, it is always good to print a copy
  Voucher for hotel with booking details
  Driver's License - In most countries both Israeli and international will ask you, bring both
  Travel insurance documents abroad - with phones and explanations for those who contact in the event of a problem
  Cash money and credit cards
